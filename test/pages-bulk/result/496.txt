   <p>We are the largest multidisciplinary international association in the
   field of pain. Founded in 1973, the International Association for the
   Study of Pain® (IASP) is a non-profit professional organization
   dedicated to furthering research on pain and improving the care of
   patients with pain.
   <p>Membership in IASP is open to scientists, physicians, dentists,
   psychologists, nurses, physical therapists, and other health
   professionals actively engaged in pain research and to those who have
   special interest in the diagnosis and treatment of pain. Currently
   IASP has more than 6,900 members from 106 countries.
