Steve Wozniak Believes Apple Should Pay Same 50% Tax Rate He Does

Posted by Killian Bell on Apr 22, 2016 | No Comments

Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak believes the Cupertino company he founded with Steve Jobs in 1976 should be paying the same 50% tax rate that he does. Woz told BBC News that it’s “unfair” for companies to pay any less.

“I do a lot of work, I do a lot of travel and I pay over 50% of anything I make in taxes and I believe that’s part of life and you should do it,” Woz said. He adds that the rate should be no different for companies like Apple.

When Woz founded Apple alongside Jobs and Ronald Wayne, he knew at the time that it would be “a worldwide company selling huge amounts of computers everywhere, and we just assumed we would pay taxes on it,” he said.

“We didn’t think we’d be figuring out how to go off to the Bahamas and have special accounts like people do to try to hide their money… And maybe the tax rates are different for a company than they are for a person, but that’s something that bothers me to this day.”

When Woz was asked about using tax havens and loopholes to maximize profits, he told the BBC he was never interested in making money — unlike Jobs, who started Apple with that goal, Woz said. “That was his big thing and that was extremely important and critical and good”

Apple is one of a number of major corporations that are being scrutinized for possible tax avoidance in Europe. In September 2014, the company was accused of receiving illegal state aid from Ireland — where its European headquarters are based — by the European Commission.

It’s thought Apple’s tax rate there could be as little as 1.8% of its overseas profits. However, at a hearing last month, Apple insisted it is the “largest taxpayer in the world,” having coughed up $13.2 billion in worldwide taxes in 2015 at the effective tax rate of 36.4%.

Tim Cook believes that the scrutiny is “total political crap.” He insists that there is no truth behind the claims that Apple avoids tax, and he’s adamant that the company “pays every tax dollar we owe.”
